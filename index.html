About Jai Bajrang Motor Driving School

Founded in 2014, Jai Bajrang Motor Driving School is a government-recognized driving institute dedicated to creating safe, confident, and responsible drivers. With experienced instructors and modern training methods, we provide professional driving lessons for beginners as well as advanced learners.

We offer training on Hatchback, Sedan, and SUV vehicles along with Simulator-Based Training for better practical learning. Our curriculum follows the Motor Vehicles Act 1988 and updated road safety standards to ensure quality driving education.
